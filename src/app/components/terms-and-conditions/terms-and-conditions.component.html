Insurance Coverage

Ghost Rentals provides insurance exclusively for licensed drivers listed in the rental agreement. The insurance policy complies with UAE car accident regulations and is available upon request.

Booking Modifications and Cancellations

You can modify or cancel your booking free of charge up to 48 hours before the rental start date.

Security Deposit
